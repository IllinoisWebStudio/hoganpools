About Hogan Pools

Why choose Hogan Pools?

Hogan Pools is a family-owned custom pool builder serving St. Louis and surrounding communities. Owner Anthony George brings more than 20 years of hands-on pool construction experience, supported by three generations of pool-building knowledge dating back to 1976.

What type of pools does Hogan Pools build?

We specialize in custom gunite swimming pools. Each pool is individually designed around your property, lifestyle, and vision rather than being limited by pre-designed shapes or sizes.

What areas does Hogan Pools serve?

Hogan Pools serves homeowners throughout the St. Louis area, including Ballwin and surrounding Missouri communities.

The Pool Construction Process

What happens during the first consultation?

We begin by understanding your goals, property, preferred features, and budget. This allows us to develop a pool design that fits your backyard and how you want to use the space.

What are the steps involved in building a pool?

  1. Consultation and planning
  2. Custom design and selections
  3. Permitting and site preparation
  4. Excavation and structural construction
  5. Plumbing and electrical installation
  6. Gunite application
  7. Tile, coping, and interior finishes
  8. Equipment startup and homeowner orientation
